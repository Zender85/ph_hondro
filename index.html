"If they tell you that you can’t cure back and joint problems after age 50, know that it’s not true!" – Prof. Wilbert Siongco, MD

Wilbert Siongco
Filipino professor, the founder of modern kinesiotherapy, an alternative method (neurology and orthopaedics) for the treatment of chronic diseases and musculoskeletal injuries, which is not focused on drugs and wearing corsets, but on the body's internal reserves and the patients’ understanding of their own body.
Professional experience: more than 30 years.

"Bear in mind this simple truth, and don’t believe anyone who says the opposite: the joints can and must be treated, even after the age of 50"

This doctor assures the world he can cure an 18-year-old case of osteochondritis in a few months. He claims he can heal an old woman with severe arthritis within 78 days. And joint pains, he says, disappear in four days through the right approach! And during his 48 years of activity, he was able to confirm all these claims in practice. Immediately after the broadcast on BBC2 (the topic was "how we can preserve our joints at any age "), the renowned professor Dr. Wilbert Siongco agreed to give us an interview

— Hello, Dr. Siongco. Please, tell us, is it true that joint problems and osteochondritis are "compulsory" in old age?

— Hello! Of course, it’s not true. I would call "compulsory" the excessive trust in those doctors , who treat you for 10 years but can never cure you. Actually, the joints, osteochondritis and the whole skeleton can be treated at any age. It’s simply science, not magic.

If you know a particular secret, and have a little discipline, you can even cure your joints at home, very quickly, and that’s exactly what thousands of my patients are doing.

— And what is this secret?

— Understanding the reason why it hurts -that is the secret. In encyclopaedias there are up to 147 possible causes of the development of osteochondritis and arthrosis, but the consequence is always the same: joints, vertebrae and cartilages lose their elasticity, and hence you feel pain. They wear out because of an inadequate blood supply.

Here what I mean: if we restore the blood supply to the joints, we cure them!

— Right, but generally people believe that after 45 years of age it’s almost impossible to restore blood supply, isn’t it?

— That is not true either! After an incident I was stuck in wheelchair, but I managed to get myself out of it. And I was older than 45.

— Have you helped others get out of their wheelchairs as well?

— Sure, many times. But most of my patients are people with ordinary "age issues". They come to me with similar problems: osteochondritis, arthrosis, arthritis, radiculitis, nerve compression. These diseases are bothersome and exhausting.

They complain that they are in pain, it’s hard for them even to walk. They are just ordinary people, neither athletes nor disabled. Sometimes they ask me, "why is this happening to me? why me?". And I answer: "Let's not whine, let’s just restore the blood supply".

— So how can you restore the blood supply at this age?

— I used to treat people with the help of gymnastics, and a set of 96 exercises on the machines...until recently. I mean, this is a very effective method, but very complicated and it takes a long time.
It's difficult for patients, and they don’t have enough time to go to the gym. I was convinced there had to be a more modern and easier method, and in the end I found it.

— That's interesting! Could you tell our readers more about it?

— Of course. Our research centre was the first in out country to receive authorized access to the newest remedy against osteochondritis and joint pain . Well, to be honest, I just laughed when I first heard about it, because I didn’t believe it could be so effective. But after we tested it, I was astonished: 4,567 people were completely cured, which was more than 94% of all pacients. 5.6% felt improvements, and only on 0.4% it had no effect.

— And what is this remedy exactly?

— I’m talking about a special remedy called «Hondrocream». This is a product that will make you say "goodbuy" to back and joint pain in the shortest time, even in just 4 days, and it is able to cure even very difficult cases within a few of months.

It’s only produced in the northwest of the country, and the Ministry of Health spent 1.3 million pounds to develop the recipe. And our centre, by the way, spent almost 200,000 pounds for the right to manufacture it here in the UK.

— And how does this magic remedy work?

— No magic, just science. The product contains herbal extracts, whose effect is complemented by glucosamine and chondroitin. These help treat joint pain and restore their functioning, without surgery.

Even after a single use you’re activating around 930,000 cells, which helps to restore the blood flow. That’s what the treatment is about. Most important here is stability.

— Sounds impressive. Please explain to us, what does this mean for ordinary people with various problems?

— This means that outdated medicine in our country is a thing of the past, and you will be able to cure yourselves at home, in just one or two months. «Hondrocream» doesn't have temporary effect, it gives the body a "restart" at the cellular level. It eliminates the root of the pain and returns the joints and spine to their original, healthy state. So the patient is able to eliminate the root of the disease: a weakened blood flow to the cartilages due to old, slow cells.

On the first day, this remedy "turns on" the regeneration systems of the body. It relieves the symptoms, which you will feel right away. In two or three weeks the treatment is already completed, and the very important thing is to prevent the return of the condition, by doing the treatment course again.

— Does «Hondrocream» help only against arthrosis and osteochondritis??

No, it acts at the cellular level and restores the blood supply. Thus, it also treats arthrosis and arthritis, gout, sciatica, rheumatism, radiculitis and osteochondritis, vertebral disk displacement. Sprains, bruises, contusions, fractures, even calluses – it’s amazingly effective. Generally it cures all diseases related to the spine.

— It’s an amazing remedy indeed. And is it really true that it heals all these conditions, and doesn’t only take the pain away?

«Hondrocream» eliminates the pain (at the early stages of the treatment) and then eliminates the condition entirely. I do love exercise and sports, and I won’t stop doing them, but for most patients this is a simpler, more efficient and more accessible method of treatment.

We want to start large-scale sales in pharmacies, but we haven’t been able to come to an agreement with the pharmacists, because Hondrocream could be bad for their business. People buy drugs and remedies from them for years, and there are more and more sufferers, and this situation benefits them.

However there are also advantages: the absence of middlemen, which allows us to keep the price 5 times lower than it is in other countries.

The delivery is made through post, with payment on delivery, and using it doesn’t require supervision by a specialist – you use it at home. You don’t need to take my word for it, you can try the remedies sold by other manufacturers. But I’m sure that you won’t find anything similar to «Hondrocream» in terms of effectiveness.

Yes, of course. You all should know, that diseases of the back and joints are "getting younger" and you need to pay attention to this problem even when you only have weak pains from time to time. The doctor won’t be the one to persuade you to treat it.
